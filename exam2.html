The Post

The Post is a 2017 American political thriller film about The Washington Post and the publication of the Pentagon Papers. Directed by Steven Spielberg, written by Liz Hannah and Josh Singer, starring Meryl Streep and Tom Hanks. Set in 1971, it depicts journalists' efforts to publish classified documents about U.S. involvement in the Vietnam War.
